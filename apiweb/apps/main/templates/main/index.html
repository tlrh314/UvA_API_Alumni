The Anton Pannekoek Institute for Astronomy of the University of Amsterdam is proud of its alumni and considers them part of the API family for all times. Because it feels connected to its former students and personnel, it remains interested in their activities and values a long-lasting contact.

These webpages provide an overview of the history of API in terms of its graduates and former staff, starting with its founder Anton Pannekoek. It provides information on their astronomical research fields, directions they have taken after leaving API, and contact information made available.

Our alumni have ventured out to all corners of the Netherlands and the world, and have taken many different walks of life. From a few, we present interviews that highlight personnel and professional developments.
