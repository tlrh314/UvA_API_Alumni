API 90 years — alumni day

Saturday November 26 2011, the celebration of 90 years Anton Pannekoek Institute was held, with a good amount of present and past "APIs" visiting.

Alumni book

For the celebration, the existing alumni book was updated, and is now available. There is a "lost and found" section, where people are listed for who contact information may be missing. If you do have information about a person there, you can let us know by contacting us: secr-astro-science@uva.nl. If you see people missing on this, again, please let us know. You can download the API alumni book (PDF).
